> **New Brunswick just launched Explora AI**, a groundbreaking 24/7 travel chatbot that's transforming how visitors explore Atlantic Canada. This AI-powered assistant answers questions instantly, suggests personalized itineraries, and helps travelers navigate the region's best attractions—anytime, anywhere.

## What Happened: Context & Timeline

New Brunswick's tourism board unveiled **Explora AI** on March 25, 2026, marking a significant shift in how Atlantic Canada welcomes international and domestic travelers. The chatbot integrates natural language processing to understand traveler preferences—whether you're seeking outdoor adventures, cultural experiences, or family-friendly attractions—and delivers real-time recommendations tailored to your interests.

The platform launched after 18 months of development in partnership with local tourism operators, hospitality businesses, and regional government agencies. Explora AI operates across web browsers, mobile apps, and messaging platforms (WhatsApp, Messenger), making it accessible to travelers in 12 languages including English, French, Mandarin, and Spanish.

According to the New Brunswick Tourism Board, the chatbot has been trained on comprehensive data covering 500+ attractions, 200+ accommodations, seasonal events, and real-time availability for activities. Users can ask questions ranging from "What's the best time to see Hopewell Rocks?" to "Where can I find gluten-free dining near Fredericton?" and receive instant, accurate answers.

## Industry Context & Analysis

Explora AI arrives at a pivotal moment for Atlantic Canada tourism. The region welcomed 3.2 million visitors in 2025, representing a 12% year-over-year increase, yet many travelers still struggle to access reliable, localized information. Traditional tourism websites often overwhelm users with generic content, while travel agents require advance booking and may charge consultation fees.

The AI chatbot addresses this gap by democratizing travel planning. Similar initiatives exist in Europe—Portugal's "Visit Portugal" AI assistant and Ireland's tourism chatbot have each processed over 500,000 queries in their first year. New Brunswick's Explora AI is positioned to capture a similar market share across Atlantic Canada.

Tourism experts note that AI-powered planning tools boost destination competitiveness by reducing friction in the pre-trip research phase. A 2025 Skift report found that 64% of leisure travelers use AI assistants for trip planning, signaling broad market readiness for New Brunswick's launch. The economic impact could be significant: improved accessibility may drive 8-15% visitor growth within 18 months, translating to $40-60 million in additional tourism revenue region-wide.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How does Explora AI help with travel Canada Atlantic planning?**
Explora AI instantly answers questions about attractions, accommodations, dining, and activities across New Brunswick and neighboring Atlantic provinces. You can ask about Hopewell Rocks tide times, Fundy National Park hiking trails, or Acadian cultural festivals, and receive personalized recommendations in seconds—available 24/7 without human agent delays.

**Can Explora AI book my hotel or activities directly?**
The chatbot provides recommendations and real-time availability but doesn't process payments directly. Instead, it links to trusted booking partners (Airbnb, Booking.com, local operators) where you complete reservations. This protects both travelers and local businesses while maintaining competitive pricing.

**Is Explora AI available on my phone while traveling in New Brunswick?**
Yes. The chatbot works on any smartphone via web browser, and dedicated apps are available on iOS and Android. It functions with or without a data plan (offline mode stores frequently accessed information), making it useful even in remote areas of the Maritimes.
