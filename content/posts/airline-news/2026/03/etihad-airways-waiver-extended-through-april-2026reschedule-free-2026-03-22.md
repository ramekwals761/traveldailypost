## What This Means for Travelers

* **Check your booking immediately:** Log into Etihad's website or mobile app using your booking reference to confirm your flight is eligible. The waiver applies to all existing reservations, but you must act before April 15 to claim it.

* **Rebook now for Eid and spring travel:** If you're considering changing travel dates around Eid al-Fitr (estimated April 2026) or want to shift to less-crowded dates, do this before the waiver expires. After April 15, standard change fees (typically $50–$300+) will apply.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I reschedule my Etihad flight multiple times before April 15?**
Yes—Etihad permits unlimited rebooking changes at no cost under this extended waiver. However, each rescheduled flight must operate before April 15, 2026. Once you confirm a new date, that flight is locked; further changes after April 15 will incur standard fees.

**Do I lose my current ticket price if I reschedule to a more expensive flight?**
No. If your new flight costs more than your original booking, Etihad absorbs the fare difference under the waiver. If it costs less, you receive a credit (non-refundable) toward future travel on Etihad or partner carriers.

**How long does it take to receive a refund, and can I get it in cash?**
Refund processing takes 7–14 business days to your original payment method (credit card, debit card, or bank account). Cash refunds at the airport counter are available in limited cases; contact Etihad's customer service to request this option.

**Can I reschedule my Etihad flight to a different airline?**
No—the waiver applies only to Etihad Airways and its codeshare partners (Air Serbia, Air Astana, others). To switch to a non-partner airline, you'll need to cancel for a refund and rebook separately.

**What happens if my new flight gets canceled after I rebook?**
You retain the same waiver rights on the new flight. If Etihad cancels the rebooked flight, you can reschedule again at no cost or request a refund—no deadlines apply to Etihad-caused cancellations.
