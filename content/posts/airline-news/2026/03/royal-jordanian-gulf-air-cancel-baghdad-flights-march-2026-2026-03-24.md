## What Happened: Context & Timeline

**Royal Jordanian (RJ)** halted its Baghdad-to-Amman service effective March 24, while **Gulf Air (GF)** simultaneously suspended Baghdad-to-Manama flights. Both airlines serve as primary connectors for Middle East business and leisure travel, making these cancellations particularly impactful during peak spring travel season.

Operational sources cite infrastructure and scheduling constraints at Baghdad International Airport as the primary cause. The suspensions affect both morning and evening departure slots, eliminating redundancy for travelers who typically had 2-3 daily options between these hubs.
