## Your Questions Answered

**Can I attend the festival without being a registered delegate?**
Most URBACT City Festival sessions are open to public registration. Check `urbact.eu` for specific panel schedules and ticketing. Some walking tours and workshops may be delegate-only, but the main events welcome engaged travelers. Day passes typically cost €35-50.

**Is it safe to visit Nicosia? Isn't the city divided?**
Nicosia is politically complex but extremely safe for travelers. The UN-patrolled buffer zone dividing the city is a curiosity rather than a danger. Turkish and Greek Cypriot communities coexist peacefully in tourist areas. Violence is virtually nonexistent. Exercise normal city awareness, and you'll have zero issues exploring both sides of the Old Town.

**What else should I see in Nicosia beyond the festival?**
The Cyprus Museum houses Mediterranean treasures. Selimiye Mosque (formerly St. Sophia Cathedral) showcases stunning architecture. The Old Town's maze of 16th-century streets rewards aimless wandering. Ledra Street—the main dividing line—is an eerie, moving experience that contextualizes the city's unique history.
