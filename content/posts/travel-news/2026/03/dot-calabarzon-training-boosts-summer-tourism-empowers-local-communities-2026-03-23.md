By equipping local workforces with certified training before this peak, the DOT aims to reduce service gaps and enhance visitor satisfaction.

These empowerment initiatives also address broader economic goals: strengthening local employment, reducing youth unemployment, and ensuring tourism revenue stays within communities rather than being captured by large metropolitan operators.
