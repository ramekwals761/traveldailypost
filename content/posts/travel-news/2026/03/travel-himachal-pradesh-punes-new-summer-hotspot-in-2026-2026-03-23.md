## Frequently Asked Questions

**When should I visit Himachal Pradesh from Pune to avoid crowds?**
Travel Himachal Pradesh in **April 1–15 or June 10–30** for the best balance of weather and fewer tourists. Peak chaos occurs May 10–25 when school holidays align nationally. Late April offers clear skies and daytime temps of 18–22°C ideal for trekking and sightseeing.

**What's the cheapest way to travel from Pune to Himachal Pradesh?**
Take the Pune-Chandigarh Shatabdi Express (`₹1,800–₹2,400`) departing 6:20 AM, arriving 4:30 PM. Connect via government HRTC buses to Shimla (4 hours, `₹400–₹600`). Total: `₹2,200–₹3,000` vs. flights at `₹4,500+`. Budget homestays in Kasol, Palampur, and Shimla range `₹1,500–₹3,000/night`.

**Is Himachal Pradesh worth visiting versus Kashmir or Uttarakhand?**
Yes, for Pune-based summer travel. Himachal edges out competitors on three metrics: accessibility (road + rail + flight all viable), cost (`₹30–₹40K` all-in vs. `₹50–₹80K` for Kashmir), and variety (15+ distinct hill towns vs. concentrated circuits in Kashmir/Uttarakhand). Choose Himachal for a balanced, budget-friendly first alpine experience; Kashmir/Sikkim for splurge-worthy, specialized trips.
