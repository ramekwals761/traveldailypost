## Your Questions Answered

**What nationalities qualify for Oman's e-visa?**
Most passports qualify—159 nationalities at current count, including US, EU, UK, Canada, Australia, and most Asian nations. Check the official [Oman e-Government Portal](https://www.rop.gov.om/en) or your airline's confirmation to verify your specific eligibility before booking.

**Is the e-visa as valid as a traditional visa stamp?**
Completely. The e-visa is recognized by all Oman immigration authorities and fulfills entry requirements identically. It appears digitally in your passport record—no physical stamp needed. Processing is faster because there's no physical handling.

**What if my e-SIM doesn't work after landing?**
Arcube provides 24/7 support via app or phone. Fallback options exist at the airport, but this is rare. Have a backup WiFi plan (hotel WiFi usually covered) while e-SIM activates, typically within 15 minutes of landing.

**Can I book the e-visa and e-SIM separately from the flight?**
Yes. But bundling through SalamAir saves 15-20% because the airline negotiates volume rates. Standalone purchases through Arcube's regular portal cost more but work identically.

**When does the e-visa start counting—booking or arrival?**
Arrival. Your 30-day tourist visa begins when immigration scans your passport at Muscat airport, not when you purchase it. Plan accordingly if you need flexibility in travel dates.
