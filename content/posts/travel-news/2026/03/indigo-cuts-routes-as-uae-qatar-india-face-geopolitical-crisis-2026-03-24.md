## What This Means for Travelers

* **Check booking status NOW:** Log into IndiGo's app or website immediately if you have bookings on DEL-DOH, BOM-DXB, or BLR-AUH routes. Many March-April flights already show "Cancelled" status.

* **Understand your refund rights:** Under IATA regulations and Indian aviation rules, you're entitled to a full refund OR rebooking on the next available flight within 7 days. Document your original booking confirmation.

* **Explore codeshare alternatives:** Check if your ticket is interline-eligible. **Air India**, **Qatar Airways**, and **Emirates** may accept IndiGo passengers on alternate routings via `DEL → IST → DOH` (Istanbul hub) or `BOM → DXB` via connecting carriers.

* **Book Asia-Pacific workarounds:** Flights via **Bangkok (BKK)**, **Kuala Lumpur (KUL)**, or **Singapore (SIN)** are operating normally. Budget 12-16 hours total travel time but avoid affected corridors entirely.

* **Lock in alternative fares immediately:** Regional carriers like **Flydubai**, **Air Arabia**, and **SpiceJet** are increasing frequencies. Fares are rising 15-25% above normal rates—book within 24 hours if rebooking is necessary.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I get a refund from IndiGo if my Qatar, India, or UAE flight is cancelled?**
Yes. Under Indian civil aviation regulations and IATA standards, IndiGo must offer a full refund within 7-14 business days OR rebooking on the next available flight at no additional cost. Submit your refund request via the IndiGo website or customer service hotline (+91-124-4171717) with your original booking confirmation and photo ID.

**What are my best alternatives if my India-to-Middle East flight is cancelled?**
Book immediately on connecting flights via Southeast Asia (Bangkok, Kuala Lumpur) using Air India or SpiceJet, or reroute via European hubs (Istanbul, Abu Dhabi) using Turkish Airlines or Etihad. Total journey time increases 4-8 hours but bypasses affected corridors. Expect fares 20-30% higher than original bookings.

**How long will IndiGo suspensions last?**
IndiGo has not announced a restart date. Industry analysts estimate 4-8 weeks of disruptions based on regional stability trends, but this could extend through Q2 2026. Monitor IndiGo's official website and IATA advisories weekly for updates.

**Am I entitled to compensation beyond refunds?**
India's aviation rules do not mandate compensation for cancellations due to geopolitical events ("force majeure" clause). However, if IndiGo offered rebooking that extended travel time beyond 24 hours, you may qualify for EU261-equivalent compensation in some jurisdictions. Consult a travel lawyer or aviation ombudsman in your country.

**Should I cancel my upcoming trip or reschedule?**
Reschedule to June 2026 or later if flexibility allows—Middle East aviation is expected to stabilize by mid-year. If travel is urgent, use Asia-Pacific routing (BKK, KUL, SIN) to reach Middle Eastern destinations. Avoid direct bookings on suspended routes; book through codeshare partners when possible.
