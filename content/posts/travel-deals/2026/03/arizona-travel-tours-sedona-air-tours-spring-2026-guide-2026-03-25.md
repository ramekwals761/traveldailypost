**Are scenic flights safe for first-time flyers anxious about small aircraft?**
Yes—small aircraft (Cessna 206, Piper Saratoga) have superior safety records to commercial jets. Operators provide pre-flight briefings, gradual altitude climbs, and smooth-flying smaller aircraft. **Can I bring a GoPro or drone on the flight?**
